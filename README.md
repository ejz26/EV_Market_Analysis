# Tesla's Next Move: Using Data Analytics to Identify the Biggest Opportunity in the EV Market

## Overview

This project explores the electric vehicle (EV) market to identify the biggest opportunity for Tesla's next product. By analyzing pricing, vehicle performance, battery technology, customer ratings, sales trends, and competitor positioning, the project uncovers underserved market segments and provides data-driven recommendations for Tesla's future vehicle strategy.

Using Python, SQL, and Power BI, the analysis transforms raw EV market data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards, statistical analysis, and market intelligence.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze current trends and growth across the EV market
- Compare Tesla with major competitors across price, range, battery capacity, and performance
- Identify underserved market segments with high growth potential
- Evaluate which vehicle features have the greatest impact on customer satisfaction and sales
- Develop custom metrics to measure vehicle value and market opportunity
- Recommend the ideal specifications for Tesla's next vehicle based on analytical findings
