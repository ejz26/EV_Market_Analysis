🚗 Tesla's Next Move: Using Data Analytics to Identify the Biggest Opportunity in the EV Market
Overview

This project analyzes the electric vehicle market to identify underserved opportunities and recommend the ideal specifications for Tesla's next vehicle. Using Python, SQL, and Power BI, the analysis explores market trends, competitor performance, customer preferences, and pricing strategies to provide data-driven business recommendations.

Objectives
Analyze trends in the EV market
Compare Tesla with major competitors
Identify underserved market segments
Determine the features that drive sales and customer satisfaction
Recommend Tesla's next vehicle based on data
